Component 6 — Family Guardian Monitoring

Parents and caregivers track dependents in real-time. Designed for children, elderly, and family members with special needs.

Live location tracking on map

Geofence alerts: home, school, coaching, playground

Route deviation notifications if path changes unexpectedly

Safe arrival confirmations when reaching destination

Scheduled check-in reminders for elderly family members

Emergency triggers from dependent's device

Privacy controls: Dependents manage who can track and when. Not surveillance — supervised safety.
